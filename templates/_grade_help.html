ⓘ How grades work

Every letter grade runs S · A · B · C · D · F (with +/–). There are three kinds, depending on where you see it:

🎯 Skill grade (absolute)

Overall skill on a fixed Halo scale, from a player's ranked-game averages — not affected by how teammates do. Weighted: Slaying/KDA 35%, Impact/damage-ratio 25%, Gunplay/accuracy 20%, Survival/avg-life 10%, Medals 10%.

Player cards · player report card · Lifetime · each match's grade
📅 Session report card

How a player did this session graded on a curve. By default (⚖️ Squad grading) it's a percentile against the whole squad's last 365 days — not the player's own history. A 👤 Self grading toggle re-grades against their own baseline instead. Either way it can differ from their skill grade.

Dashboard “Squad Session Report Card”
📊 Ranking grade (vs squad)

Where a player ranks against the other tracked players in that one table. S = top 10% of the squad, B = middle of the pack.

Comparison tables: maps, matchups, objectives, veto…

On a player's game-by-game breakdown you'll see two grades — they answer different questions and can disagree:

🎮 Games · avg (absolute)

The average of your individual per-game grades. Each game scores on a fixed scale — KDA 60%, accuracy 22%, damage dealt/taken 18%, ±3 for the win or loss — then averaged. Answers “how well did I play each game?” — independent of the lobby or how the squad usually plays.

📅 Session grade (on a curve)

75% performance + 25% result. The 75% scores the session's win-correlated stats (KDA, damage±, life, objective, score%, dmg/min) as a percentile vs the squad's last 365 days; the 25% is the session's actual win rate. Answers “how did the night go, graded on our own curve?”

So a strong Games · avg with a lower Session grade usually means you individually played well but lost — or your win-correlated numbers were only average for this squad. Grinding out ugly wins can flip it the other way.

Letter scale: S top 10% · A top 25% · B middle/average · C below · D–F well below. Hover any grade on the site to see the exact numbers behind it.
